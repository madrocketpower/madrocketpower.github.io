odyssey whale watch

I'm a deckhand on the Odyssey Whale Watch out of Portland, Maine. Some day I hope to become a naturalist for the crew. I love being out on the water and "meeting" so many of the beautiful creatures in the Gulf of Maine. Come take a trip with us!

undiscovered maine

Undiscovered Maine is a research project sponsored by the Maine Business School at UMaine Orono. I help maintain the webpage, which aims to promote economic development and tourism in more rural parts of my home state. I also frequently manage the project's social media accounts. It's really nice to be able to write about and raise awareness for the outdoor opportunities that Maine has to offer. Learn more.

animal refuge league

I volunteered at the Animal Refuge League of Greater Portland for over a year. I was responsible for caring for, feeding, and administering medicine to cats of all ages. I frequent the shelter, and encourage everyone who is considering getting a pet to adopt a rescue! Visit their site.
